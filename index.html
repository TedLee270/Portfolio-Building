About Me

Greetings! I’m Yunseong — a recent Computer Science graduate from the University of Texas at San Antonio, CompTIA Security+ certified, and aspiring IT professional with a passion for cybersecurity. My career goal is to become a penetration tester or red teamer, helping organizations strengthen their security by thinking like an attacker.

Born in Seoul, South Korea, I’ve lived in various states across the U.S., which taught me adaptability early on. I didn’t originally plan to study computer science, but after switching majors and colleges last minute, I discovered my passion for technology — a passion that only grew through countless late nights of coding, debugging, and learning how systems work from the inside out.

Balancing life as a husband and father to two young boys while completing my degree challenged me to master time management, organization, and resilience. Outside of work and study, I enjoy spending time with my family, exploring new tools, and building hands-on projects to sharpen my technical skills.

I’m excited to continue my journey in IT and cybersecurity, embracing each challenge as an opportunity to grow and become a valuable asset to the community!

Thank you for taking the time to visit my website and I hope that you found them informative. If you’d like to collaborate, share ideas, or connect, I’d be happy to hear from you!
